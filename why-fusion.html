WHY FUSION?

With established power grids and infrastructure, its easy to ask why
we would even make the effort to switch our energy. To understand,
we must look at some of the main benefits.

VIRTUALLY LIMITLESS AND CLEAN ENERGY


The main source of fuel, deuterium can be produced from seawater, and tritium can be produced from the fusion reaction in naturally abundant lithium.

Additionally, these reactions are not expected to leave nuclear waste like our current power plants, meaning their energy isn't just massive, but doesn't harm the environment either.

POWER TO FUEL OUR EVER HUNGRY DEMANDS


In theory, with just a few grams of fusion fuel, it is possible to create enough energy to supply one person in a developed country with energy for 60 years! If we develop this technology on a large scale, it would dissolve the demand for energy instantly.
