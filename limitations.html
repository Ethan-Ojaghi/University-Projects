LIMITATIONS

While we understand the mechanism and design of fusion reactors,
there are some hurdles we have yet to overcome before we can
reach our limitless paradise.

FUNDING OUR FUEL IS CURRENTLY TOO MUCH


While the cost of the fuel is low, the cost of the machine and maintenance is significant which is a large risk for most to take on energy that hasnâ€™t had any true results yet.

TEMPERATURES NEED TO BE HOTTER THAN THE SUN


For us to be able to harness fusion energy here on Earth, we would need the reactor to be able to reach temperatures of over 100 million degrees Celsius. This is six and a half times hotter than the core of the Sun. Producing such temperatures will prove difficult for us.
